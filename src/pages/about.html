About us

Chessons was established in 1992 as an Accountancy practice and has witnessed steady and progressive growth over the years. We have 20 years of experience and expertise in helping a wide variety of clients from a broad range of sectors to meet their immediate, short and long term objectives.

At Chessons, we believe in offering personalised and flexible services. Irrespective of size and nature of your business, we believe that as your Accountant, we should be a vital and active part of your team, ensuring that you succeed. We are here to guide and support you in maximising your business potentials, ensure early and speedy compliance with the Tax Office and Companies House requirements.

Every client, regardless of size, can count on the personal involvement of a lead client service partner/associate. This strong partner/associate involvement is central to our service philosophy and delivery. Our strategy is to provide tailor made financial solutions by understanding our clients’ business needs and by building a lasting professional relationship that allows us to grow and develop as you, our clients also grow and develop.

We work diligently with our clients to identify high risk areas and develop constructive and practical ways to deal with them. We communicate regularly and provide information in a form our clients can use to make effective and profitable business decisions.

Our clients come from a diverse range of backgrounds. We provide the needs of small to medium size organisations; sole traders, partnerships, charities, associations, limited liability companies, start ups. We recognise that each of our clients is unique and valued and therefore treat according to their needs.

We always give a quote for each job and reach an agreement before we start. As we are very competitive, we enjoy high clients’ retention and satisfaction.
